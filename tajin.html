Tajin

Description

Tajin is a delicious and aromatic Moroccan stew typically made with a combination of meat, vegetables, and spices. The meat is typically slow-cooked until tender and flavorful, while the vegetables add texture and nutrients to the dish. Spices like cumin, coriander, and cinnamon are used to add warmth and depth to the flavor profile, while preserved lemons and olives provide a tangy and briny contrast. Tajin is a hearty and satisfying meal that is perfect for a cozy night in with family or friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) of beef or lamb, cut into cubes
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup (150g) of green beans, trimmed
  • 1 cup (150g) of carrots, sliced
  • 1 cup (150g) of potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/2 cup (100g) of pitted green olives
  • 1/4 preserved lemon, chopped
  • 2 tbsp (30ml) of olive oil
  • 2 tsp (10g) of ground cumin
  • 1 tsp (5g) of ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp (2g) of ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp (2g) of paprika
  • 1/4 tsp (1g) of turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste

How to prepare

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the meat and cook until browned on all sides.
  2. Add the onions and garlic to the pot and saut√© until the onions are soft and translucent.
  3. Add the tomatoes, green beans, carrots, potatoes, olives, preserved lemon, and spices to the pot. Stir to combine.
  4. Add enough water to the pot to cover the ingredients by about 1 inch (2.5 cm).
  5. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 1-2 hours, or until the meat is tender and the vegetables are cooked through.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed with salt and pepper.
