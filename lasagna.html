Lasagne

Description

A beautiful lasagna is a classic Italian dish made of stacked layers of tender pasta sheets, rich meaty ragù, creamy béchamel sauce, and melted cheese. The aroma of freshly baked lasagna fills the room, while the golden brown cheese on top is bubbling and crispy. With each bite, the layers of flavors blend perfectly, creating a savory and satisfying meal that is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) of lasagne sheets
  • 2 lbs (900g) of ragù sauce
  • 1 cup (250g) of ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup (100g) of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup (100g) of grated mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tbsp (30ml) of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

How to prepare

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook lasagne sheets until al dente. Drain and rinse under cold water.
  3. In a mixing bowl, stir together ricotta cheese, half of the grated Parmesan cheese, and salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Spread a layer of ragù sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  5. Add a layer of lasagne sheets on top of the sauce, slightly overlapping each sheet.
  6. Spoon half of the ricotta mixture over the lasagne sheets, spreading it evenly.
  7. Add another layer of lasagne sheets on top of the ricotta mixture, again slightly overlapping each sheet.
  8. Spoon another layer of ragù sauce over the lasagne sheets, spreading it evenly.
  9. Repeat layers of lasagne sheets, ricotta mixture, and ragù sauce until all ingredients are used, making sure the top layer is ragù sauce.
  10. Sprinkle the remaining grated Parmesan and grated mozzarella cheese on top of the lasagne.
  11. Drizzle olive oil over the cheese.
